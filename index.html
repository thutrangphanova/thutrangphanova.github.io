Story




Khoa is visiting his grandpa and aunt for the summer break and expects another boring summer in the sleepy village. He meets up with his childhood friend Mung and together they experience a summer that they will never forget. In two short months they fall for girls for the first time, become knights, experience loss, question life and what it means to grow up, and ends up bringing the whole village together.

Description
It is a gentle film that moves gracefully with naturalistic setting and characters, taking the audience back to their own childhood. It portrays Vietnam and its people in a compassionate and loving manner, with deep sense of nostalgia for days of innocence. At the core, this story is about universal humanity that connects us all no matter where we come from.
